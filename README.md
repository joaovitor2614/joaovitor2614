## ◈ About

Geophysicist graduated from **Universidade Federal Fluminense** with extensive experience building technology solutions — particularly at the intersection of **geosciences and software engineering**. I design and develop tools that bring scientific rigor and modern engineering together, from well log interpretation platforms to full-stack web applications.

> *Turning subsurface data and business logic into elegant, working software.*
